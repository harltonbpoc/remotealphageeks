What we do

Remote Alpha Geeks provides weekly huddles/coaching sessions with the team members to discuss Wins and Challenges and ensure they get the best support they need. We will provide training where necessary including basic Microsoft Office or Google Workspace training, Outlook, Email Management & Etiquette, Calendar Management, Canva, Data Entry, etc.
